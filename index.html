about

SET Technologies is a start up company focusing on building and development of real estate, information technologies, engineering and finance. Our mission is to enable businesses in Ethiopia for adoption and enterprise-level usage of technologies. Our diverse team, industry partnerships, and investments will ignite the potential for creating a better Ethiopia by transferring technology.

With a strong, diverse team of professionals and developers representing a variety of discipline, SET Technologies’s team is ideally resourced to become a leading company in US and aspiring to foster adoption of technologies to developing countries primarily in Ethiopia. Our team is based out of Washington DC, USA, with additional members in other states and countries.

Markets

Our clients call upon us to work on their hardest problems. SET Technologies is committed to solving our clients’ toughest challenges, and we work with a diverse base of public and private sector clients across a wide swath of industries, in the U.S. and Ethiopia.

Government

Bringing efficiency and modernization to the developing countries particularily in Ethiopia by collaborating with government agencies to help citizens thrive. This includes real estate development, engineering desing, consulting and manufacturing.

Information Technology

SET Technologies works to strike the right balance in modernization by implementing complex IT and application solutions aligned with organizational objectives, information security compliance, and change management best practices.

Healthcare

Technology can help us live healthier, more affordable and better lives. But there’s much work to be done. SET Technologies is committed to helping healthcare organizations across the private and public sectors navigate their rapidly changing environments and complex markets to drive more effective treatment and approaches.

Financial Services

We help clients government agencies and industries in developing countries improve operations, mitigate risk, in an ever increasingly complex landscape. We bring a rich experience of delivering strategic and technological solutions to government financial services, banks, insurance companies, and payment processors.
